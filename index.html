Illager World War:

Adds WW1 inspired mobs for villagers and pillagers to wage war between eachother. Works on Aternos. Current Version: 1.2.1.

TFTH:

Adds the SCP-610 into the game including mobs and block infection. Works on Aternos. Current Version: 1.1b

Library Necessary for multiple mods including TFTH and Illager World War. Works on Aternos. Current Version: 4.8.3.
